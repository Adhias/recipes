Cauliflower Shaak¶

  • 1 Cauliflower

  • 1 Potato

  • 4 tablespoons Oil

  • \(\frac{1}{4}\) teaspoon Hing (Asafotida)

  • \(\frac{1}{4}\) teaspoon Turmeric Power

  • 1 teaspoon Salt

  • 1 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder

  • 1 tablespoon Dhanajiru Powder

Instructions¶

  1. Cut potato and cauliflower into small pieces.

  2. Heat oil in a pan or a wok on medium heat

  3. Add hing, potato pices and 3 tablespoons of water

  4. Cover the pan and let it boil for 4 minutes

  5. Add cauliflower pieces, salt

  6. Cover the pan again to let it cook while tossing every two minutes

  7. Once the contents have cooked, add turmeric, dhanajiru, and chilli powders

  8. Cover the pot for another 4 minutes

  9. Uncover, mix the contents and let it simmer for 2 minutes

  10. Turn off the stove, the Shaak should now be ready
