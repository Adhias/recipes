Fajitas¶

Black Beans¶

  • 1 can Black Beans

  • 3 tablespoon chopped Onion

  • 1 teaspoon Taco Seasoning

  1. Add chopped onions and saute it for 3 to 4 minutes

  2. Rinse and drain black beans

  3. Heat two tablespoons of olive oil

  4. When onions turn golden brown, add black beans

  5. Add taco seasoning and mix well

  6. Saute for 3 to 4 more minutes

Avocado Dip (Guacamole)¶

  • 1 Avocado

  • 1 Tomato

  • 1 Cilantro

  • 3 Onion slices, chopped

  • \(\frac{1}{4}\) teaspoon Salt

  • 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice

  • \(\frac{1}{4}\) teaspoon Chilli Powder

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl using a fork

  2. Add chopped onions, cilantro, salt, and lemon juice

Vegetables¶

  • 1 Onion

  • 1 Red Pepper

  • 1 Green Pepper

  • 1 Yellow Pepper

  • 1 Zucchini

  • 2 Jalapeno

  • 1 Carrrot

  • 1 cup sliced Mushroom

  • Salt

  • Crushed Black Pepper

  1. Slice thin all vegetables: peppers, carrots, zucchini, jalapeno and onion

  2. Heat up olive oil in a large pan.

  3. Add onion slices and saute for 2 to 3 minutes

  4. Add sliced carrots, saute for 5 to 7 minutes

  5. Add all peppers and zucchini

  6. Saute everything on medium heat for 10 minutes or until everything looks cooked

  7. Garnish with salt and black pepper
