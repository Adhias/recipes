Enchilada¶

Enchilada Sauce¶

  • 1 can traditional Pasta Sauce

  • \(\frac{1}{2}\) teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes

  • \(\frac{1}{2}\) teaspoon Sugar

  • \(\frac{1}{4}\) teaspoon Garlic Paste

  • \(\frac{1}{2}\) teaspoon Taco Seasoning

  1. Make enchiladata sauce by mixing all ingredients

Enchilada¶

  • 1 cup shredded Lettuce

  • Wheat Tortilla

  • Mexican Cheese

  • Sour Cream

  • 4 tablespoon Olive Oil

  • Salsa

  1. Prepare guacamole, black beans and grilled vegetables as described in receipe for fajitas

  2. Place a tortilla in a microwavable plate; spread black beans and grilled vegetables on it

  3. Roll the tortilla, pour enchilada sauce and sprinkle mexican cheeese on it before microwaving it for about a minute

  4. Garnish the tortilla with guacomole, shredded lettuce, mushroom, sour cream and jalapeno
